The Organism

Neural pixel art · Cellular automata · Generative sound

A living system where a small neural network learns to generate 16×16 pixel art tiles. Selected pieces become seeds for four modes — Game of Life, Quad Fusion, Reaction-Diffusion, and Morphogenesis — that evolve on screen while an audio engine synthesizes sound from cell activity in real time.
